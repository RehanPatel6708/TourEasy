Hong Kong:

Basic information:

It is a vibrant city located on the southern coast of China. The history of Hong Kong is what makes the people visiting shocked. Moreover, Hong Kong is known for its impressive skyline, bustling harbor and a rich blend of Eastern and Western cultures.

Attractions:

  • Hong Kong Disneyland
  • Ocean Park
  • Victoria Peak
  • Ngong Ping 360
  • Star Ferry
  • Lantau Island
