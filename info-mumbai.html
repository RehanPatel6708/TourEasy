Mumbai

Basic Information:

Mumbai is a rapidly developing city located in India. It is the capital of Maharashtra and is one of the most populated yet economically significant cities. It is known for its rich culture, street food, and is also well known or referred to as the home of “Bollywood”. Mumbai has the most diverse cuisine ranging from street food to luxurious restaurants. And to top it off, it has various different places to visit such as the Gateway of India.

Attractions:

Mumbai has various different attractions which one can visit in their journey to Mumbai, and below are a few of them:

  • Imagica
  • Gateway of India
  • Marine Drive
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus
  • Shree Siddhivinayak Temple
  • Bandra-Worli Sea Link
  • Girgaon Chowpatty beach

Best Time to Visit:

Mumbai sometimes can tend to get hot so the best time to visit would be between November to February. It would be the time where the weather would be the best in Mumbai and would be great to explore Mumbai.

Recommended Clothes:

Even if you are visiting during winters, take just a couple of jackets. Other than that, prefer to take clothes which will keep you cool and not hot. Since the weather in the morning and evening would be cold, but in the afternoon, it would be pretty hot.

Duration of Visit:

I would say around 5-7 days. A week would be best for you to explore Mumbai since if you spend more than this time, then you might get bored in Mumbai or if you stay less than this many days, you might not be able to explore Mumbai properly and try its delicious street food.
