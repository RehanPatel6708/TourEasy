Pondicherry:

Basic information:

Pondicherry, which is now known as Puducherry is a charming place located on the southeastern coast of India. It is known for its unique blend of Indian and French cultures, tranquil beaches, and amazing historical architecture. There might not be many places to visit; however, there are tons of beaches where you can relax.

Attractions:

  • Rock Beach
  • The Basilica of the Sacred Heart of Jesus
  • Paradise Beach
  • Varadaraja Perumal Temple
  • Auroville

Best time to visit:

The best time to visit Pondicherry would be during winters. Since Pondicherry can tend to be really hot during the summers, winter would be a good option to visit Pondicherry.

Recommended clothes:

I would prefer for you to take normal clothes. However, you can keep a few warm clothes since it may get cold. So for that, you can keep a couple of jackets in your bag.

How many days to visit in Pondicherry:

I would prefer 3-5 days since there is nothing much to do in Pondicherry. However, it is a good place to relax, so if you have selected a good resort, you can relax and enjoy the beaches present in Pondicherry.
