Phuket

Basic Information:

Phuket is a city with multiple different islands. It is even called the island destination. Phuket has a proper balance of culture and modern life. Phuket has many different temples and has a rich cultural heritage. However, there is even a modern life too which can be seen at night where you can experience the vibrant nightlife.

Attractions:

  • The Big Buddha
  • Bangla Road
  • Banana Beach
  • Kata Beach
  • Andamanda Phuket
  • Naka Market

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Phuket would be at the start of summers since during that time, the weather conditions are perfect for beach activities and the overall weather is great. Specifically, you can visit Phuket between January to April.

Recommended Clothes:

I would suggest taking clothes that will help you keep cool since there are chances that it might be hot there. However, for safety, do pack 1 or 2 jackets too.

Duration of Visit:

Firstly, Phuket is a place where you can chill on beaches or at resorts, so this is kind of dependent on you. But if you ask me, one can visit Phuket for around 4-5 days. During this time, they can visit different beaches and will help freshen up oneâ€™s mind.
