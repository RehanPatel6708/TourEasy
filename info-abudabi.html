Abu Dhabi

Basic Information:

Abu Dhabi is the capital city of the United Arab Emirates and is the largest of the 7 emirates. It is the perfect example of balance between modernity and Emirati culture. Abu Dhabi has to offer multiple cultural places such as the iconic Sheikh Zayed Mosque and world-famous Ferrari World.

Attractions:

There are multiple attractions in Abu Dhabi; however, few of them stand out the most. Below is the list of the attractions:

  • Ferrari World
  • Qasr Al Watan
  • Warner Bros
  • Yas Mall
  • Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque
  • Yas Waterworld
  • The Cronchie

Best Time to Visit:

One should prefer to go to Abu Dhabi during the winter months. Since Abu Dhabi is known to be a hot city, going during the winter months would be the best since the weather would be pleasant. You can visit Abu Dhabi between November and February.

Recommended Clothes:

During the day, the weather can be great so a T-shirt or shirt with jeans and shorts would be best for males and a few summer dresses for females would be best. However, carry a few warm clothes such as jackets and sweaters since in the evening, night, and early morning, it might be cold.

Expectations and Experience:

We visited Abu Dhabi during the summers, but for some reason, it wasn't hot there. I would prefer you all to go during winters since you never know how hot it can get during summers. For me, Abu Dhabi was a great experience, and we tried and visited almost everything such as the Ferrari World and Yas Waterworld. We stayed in a 5-star hotel in Yas Island, Abu Dhabi which cost us around 9000 rupees per night per person. Overall, you can expect a lot of enjoyment in Abu Dhabi, and it was a great experience for me.
